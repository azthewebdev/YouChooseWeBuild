Building Services

We at You Choose We Move take great pleasure in our standing as a dependable and respectable construction firm. We are equipped to carry out loft conversions, house additions, property restorations, and new construction to the highest standards. Our London-based builders approach every project with a warm, approachable, and highly professional attitude.

Our construction business offers a wide range of services. We customise our approach to fit any requirement since we have the specific specialists needed to handle every component of small- and large-scale construction work. We offer the kind of well-rounded workforce necessary to produce solutions on schedule, within budget, and in accordance with any specification.

Here, we look more closely at the numerous services that our craftsmen and builders offer to customers.

Home Extensions

For house additions, You Choose We Build offers complete design-and-build packages. Our construction team is equally adept at designing single- and double-story expansions for a variety of purposes. A new bedroom, a home office, a utility room, or an expanded kitchen are some of the most popular possibilities. There are no boundaries to what we can do with our construction business.

The resale value of properties is increased by home additions as well. This increase could climb by as much as 20%, depending on where you live and how you use your extra living area.

LOFT CONVERSION

For producing loft conversions of the highest caliber, our contractors have acquired a solid reputation. We consult carefully with each client to ascertain their precise needs and goals for their new living space. With a clear understanding of the objectives and desires, we provide time-tested advise and draw on our knowledge to accomplish loft conversions that not only live up to but surpass expectations.

We perfectly incorporate features like staircases, dormer windows, and Velux windows into your existing property by effortlessly integrating them. Similar to home additions, loft conversions significantly raise a property's resale value.

PROPERTY RENOVATION

As a construction company, a sizable portion of our work entails home improvements. We have the staff to handle every project internally, whether it is a single room or a full house. We work with experienced, reliable carpenters, plumbers, electricians, plasterers, joiners, and general handymen to complete home renovations in accordance with all applicable building codes.

We can transform outdated and decaying structures into residences that are beautiful, stylish, and comfortable thanks to our extensive renovation and retrofitting experience.

NEW BUILDS

You Choose We Build oversees brand-new construction projects from beginning to end. This covers the administration of site surveys, communication with the planning division of your local government, design work, and actual construction. Our craftsmen and builders adhere to architectural standards and successfully complete projects in accordance with UK Building Regulations on the first try. We just maintain uninterrupted and timely progress on projects.

Large-scale projects represented by new construction are ones that not all builders are equipped to handle right now. Our construction company thrives on the challenges that new construction presents since we have a variety of specialised builders and trades at our disposal.
